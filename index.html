About

Hey, I’m Kurt. I specialise in freelance digital design, motion graphics, and web development.

I currently work as a front-end developer at AEI, an independent electronic music company. I support huge brands such as UKF, Drum&BassArena, NCS, Subsoul, and El Dorado Festival to name a few. I also do freelance work for a range of clients from designing packaging for wooden spoons to animating stage visuals for events.

I build websites using plain ol’ HTML / CSS / JS, Wordpress, and Shopify. But i’m more than just a developer. I also do graphic design, artworking, build Messenger chatbots, make animations, motion graphics, edit videos, and coordinate multi-camera live streams.
