BuyBits

BuyBits is an online shop which specialises in hybrid mounting solutions for both motorcycles and cars creating and selling holders for mobile devices such as tablets and phones. They asked me to design a new logo to fit alongside the redesign of their website as they wanted to update their appearance and look more moden. The logo was designed to be used on screen and for print based media.

I edited some of the characters in the logo using Adobe Illustrator to look as though they fit together, connoting the idea of the products they sell all fitting together.
